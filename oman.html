Ramlat al-Wahiba (Wahiba Sand). Desert Region in Oman.

The area is occupied by Bedouins (nomads) predominantly from the tribe of al-Wahiba for whom the region is named. They congregate at Al Huyawah, an oasis near the border of the desert, between June and September to gather dates.
